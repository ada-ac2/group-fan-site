Fun Facts

  • Domesticated individuals can be taught to speak.
  • Noisy species with a unique squawking call.
  • In the Netherlands, these birds are considered pests!

Regular Facts

  • These birds are sexually dimorphic.
  • The average wing length for a Rose-ringed Parakeet is 15 to 17.5cm.
  • Their diet consists of fruits, veggies, berries, nuts, and seeds.
